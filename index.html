Everyday life from the past to the present

How living have changed from past to present

Introduce your culture using words and picture, and put stickers on the paper together. Popular works will remain untill the end of the event.

  • Summary:This event introduces how people in the past lived in their homes and how modern housing has developed over time. It also explores the diversity of climate, culture, and values reflected in differences in housing. Participants will have the opportunity to reflect on the characteristics of their own homes and regions while experiencing the charm and history of housing around the world.
  • Schedule:September 15th (Monday), 19th (Friday), 2025
  • Time:All days 10:00-16:00
  • Fee:Free (separate admission fee to the main building required)
