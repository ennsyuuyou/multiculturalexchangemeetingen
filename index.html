Everyday life from the past to the present

Let's compare food, clothing, and shelter in various countries!!

Introduce your culture using words and picture, and put stickers on the paper together. Popular works will remain untill the end of the event.

  • Summary:The essential elements for human life—“clothing, food, and shelter”—have taken many forms, changing greatly depending on the culture, natural environment, and social circumstances of the time and place. In this event, under the theme “Then and Now of Clothing, Food, and Shelter,” we will explore changes in lifestyles around the world and deepen our understanding of people’s ingenuity and diversity in daily life.
